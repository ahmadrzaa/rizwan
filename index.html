About Our Shop

Welcome to Rizwan Shop, Bangalore's premier destination for top-quality second-hand clothing.

Nestled in the heart of the bustling city, our shop offers a diverse array of pre-loved garments that not only embrace sustainable fashion but also allow you to add unique and vintage styles to your wardrobe without breaking the bank.

we believe that fashion should be accessible and environmentally friendly. Each piece in our collection is carefully selected and inspected to ensure it meets our high standards of quality and style.

Join us in our mission to reduce fashion waste and discover the endless possibilities that second-hand clothing offers at Rizwan Shop,Bangalore.
